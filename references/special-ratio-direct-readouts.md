# Wu Boshi Special Ratio Direct Readouts

This file defines the move:

- once a controlling ratio matches a remembered special ratio, read the target directly

## Core Principle

Some values are easier read from a known ratio family than re-derived every time.

Examples:

- `3:4:5`
- `1:1:sqrt(2)`
- `1:sqrt(3):2`
- golden-ratio triangles
- clean eccentricity or slope-ratio families

## Trigger

Use this when:

- the geometry has already been compressed to one controlling triangle or ratio
- the answer becomes a direct readout from a memorized special family

## Fast Move

1. Identify the controlling ratio.
2. Match it to a stable remembered family.
3. Read the target directly and pay one small trust bill.

## Hard Rule

Do not force a special ratio.
The family must be visible from the reduced picture, not from wishful pattern-matching.
