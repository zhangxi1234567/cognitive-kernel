# Compat-Active Informative Divergence

This file defines the compatibility-active form of informative divergence.

Its purpose is:

- preserve small local differences between nearby candidates long enough to matter
- avoid premature flattening of the field into one undifferentiated route

## Core Claim

Compat-only execution should already allow:

- nearby candidates to differ in what burden they illuminate
- that difference to remain locally visible
- consolidation to happen only after the difference stops paying for itself

This is not:

- maintaining parallel full routes

It is:

- preserving informative contrast

## What This Means In Compatibility Form

At the compatibility-active level, informative divergence means:

- two nearby candidates may stay alive because they are not redundant yet
- one may stress the seal while another stresses the carrier
- one may cool only after its distinctive burden has been absorbed

## Allowed Compatibility Pressure

The compatibility layer may already preserve:

- `informative_divergence`
- `neighbor_pressure`
- `consolidation_gravity`

These are enough to support:

- weak local contrast

without:

- explicit compare-all behavior

## Final Rule

Compat-only execution should already preserve local candidate differences while they still delete different burden.

It must not expose this as a required comparison ritual.
