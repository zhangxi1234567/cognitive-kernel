## Main Reduction Logic Extracted

## 1. Externalize First

Observed behavior:

- hard symbolic shells are repeatedly turned into pictures, graphs, triangles, side views, or section views
- geometry and function problems are both pushed into visual structure before long algebra

Bottom-layer logic:

- if the representation is expensive, change representation before increasing computation
- the picture is not decoration; it is the first low-cost readout of the control relation

Mechanism family:

- `Draw First`
- `Let The Figure Speak`
- `Projection Readout`

## 2. Use The Definition As A Direct Weapon

Observed behavior:

- many conic and function questions are attacked by "看见定义就直接上定义"
- the notes repeatedly convert abstract conditions back into the defining relation before secondary machinery

Bottom-layer logic:

- do not treat the definition as background theory
- treat it as the shortest bridge back to the controlling relation

Mechanism family:

- `Definition-First Trigger`
- `Relation Before Object`
- `Target-Only Control`

## 3. Collapse The Object To One Readout

Observed behavior:

- area, volume, conic, and vector questions are repeatedly reduced to one length, one gap, one radius, one support line, one section, or one projection
- several problems are not solved by reconstructing the whole object, but by reading one decisive observable

Bottom-layer logic:

- thick object -> thin readout
- many quantities -> one controlling quantity
- full relation web -> one load-bearing measurement

Mechanism family:

- `Area To Line Readout`
- `Projection Readout`
- `Container To Section`

## 4. Boundary And Extreme Positions Expose The Hinge

Observed behavior:

- the notes repeatedly use endpoint, vertex, tangent, `e -> 1`, `e -> 0`, `θ -> 0`, and other extreme configurations
- the edge case is used to reveal the range, regime split, or answer shape

Bottom-layer logic:

- the easiest dangerous edge often exposes the same mechanism more clearly than the interior does
- boundary is not only for final checking; boundary is often the route finder

Mechanism family:

- `Local Seam Controls Global`
- `Boundary / Extreme Methods`
- `Special-Value Probing`

## 5. Special Values Are Used As Structural Probes

Observed behavior:

- many solutions try `0`, `1`, symmetry values, equal sides, linear functions, constant sequences, or near-boundary values first
- these are not random guesses; they are used to test which structure survives

Bottom-layer logic:

- special values are witness probes
- a good special value is chosen because it stresses the mechanism

Mechanism family:

- `Puzzle Assignment`
- `Special-Value Probing`
- `Guess Then Verify`

## 6. Build Or Call A Model Instead Of Solving Raw

Observed behavior:

- the notes repeatedly say `模型`
- many hard problems are routed into named geometric, functional, or combinatorial models before detailed solving
- examples include symmetry models, flagpole models, cross-plane models, function symmetry models, and standard conic triangles

Bottom-layer logic:

- a model is a compressed carrier of the controlling relation
- once the model is called correctly, most of the novelty dies

Mechanism family:

- `Match To A Mother Object`
- `Canonical Normalization`
- `Function Archetype Matching`

## 7. Convert Algebraic Conditions Into Geometry Or Motion

Observed behavior:

- vector dot products become length or angle relations
- derivative or function behavior becomes graph movement, turning points, symmetry, or monotone intervals
- path and distance questions become reflection models

Bottom-layer logic:

- when symbolic operations hide the control mechanism, recode them as geometry, motion, or graph behavior

Mechanism family:

- `Transformation Rewrite`
- `Dot Product By Projection`
- `Math-Physics Bridge`

## 8. Symmetry Is Used To Delete Fake Degrees Of Freedom

Observed behavior:

- symmetry is by far the most repeated method signal in the corpus
- many ranges, extrema, and constructions are first collapsed to symmetric or near-symmetric positions

Bottom-layer logic:

- symmetry is not merely a nice property
- it is a variable-killer
- it removes fake distinctions and reduces case count

Mechanism family:

- `Symmetry Clusters`
- `Collapse The Dimension`
- `Find The Controlling Quantity`

## 9. Compute Only After Route Compression

Observed behavior:

- the notes repeatedly say to avoid complex computation if geometry, models, special values, or direct readout already settle most of the work
- calculation is often used as a seal, not as the route itself

Bottom-layer logic:

- compute residue, not the whole problem
- if structure already killed 80 percent of the burden, only verify the hinge

Mechanism family:

- `Compare Without Calculating`
- `Minimal Verification`
- `Answer First Then Backfill`

## 10. Guess The Answer Shape, Then Backfill Only Enough

Observed behavior:

- explicit `先猜后证`
- explicit "先猜答案后补过程"
- repeated exam advice to get the route or answer shape first, then pay the minimum proof bill

Bottom-layer logic:

- guessing is used as search compression
- verification must target the fragile hinge, not rebuild the full standard derivation

Mechanism family:

- `Guess Then Verify`
- `Answer-Shape Pressure`
- `Rapid Verification`

## Extracted Meta-Principles

These notes strongly support the following deeper meta-principles:

### A. Representation Before Computation

When the problem is hard, first ask whether the representation is wrong.

### B. Relation Before Object

Do not reconstruct the whole object if one relation already controls the target.

### C. Thin Controller Behind Thick Target

Many area, volume, graph, or conic questions are secretly controlled by a lower-dimensional observable.

### D. Boundary Reveals Mechanism

Extreme configurations are not only checks; they are often the cleanest window into the mechanism.

### E. Model Calling Beats Raw Grinding

A correct model kills novelty earlier than a correct derivation does.

### F. Symmetry Deletes Work

Symmetry is a computation killer and case-count reducer.

### G. Verification Should Be Local

Once the route is compressed, verification should attack the seam, not replay the chapter.
