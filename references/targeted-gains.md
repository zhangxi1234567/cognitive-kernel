## Saturation Note

Current public Bilibili evidence appears close to saturation for broad scraping.

What public surfaces still provide:

- stronger title confirmation
- adjacent snippet leakage
- occasional extra direct video pages

What public surfaces are not yielding:

- full hidden list contents
- full collection enumeration
- robust transcript-level extraction

This means future scraping should be treated as marginal unless it targets a specific missing family.
