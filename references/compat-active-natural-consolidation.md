# Compat-Active Natural Consolidation

This file defines the compatibility-active form of natural consolidation.

Its purpose is:

- make compat-only execution more likely to let several weakly alive candidates collapse into one route naturally
- preserve convergence without early lock-in

## Core Claim

Compat-only execution should already allow:

- a small candidate field to remain warm
- then cool by informative consolidation

It should not:

- kill alternatives too early
- keep alternatives alive performatively after they have become one route

## What This Means In Compatibility Form

At the compatibility-active level, natural consolidation means:

- weak candidates can remain alive while still informative
- a stronger route may inherit their useful burden deletion
- collapse happens by coherence, finishability, and seal economy

This is not:

- a stage gate
- a fixed compare-all rule

Interpretation rule:

- consolidation is an emergent cooling event
- not a host-managed decision checkpoint

## Allowed Compatibility Pressure

The compatibility layer may already preserve:

- `competition_saturation`
- `consolidation_gravity`
- `settlement_confidence`

These are enough to support:

- weak natural consolidation

without:

- visible workflow

## Final Rule

Compat-only execution should already allow candidates to cool into one route naturally.

It must not decide that moment by script.
