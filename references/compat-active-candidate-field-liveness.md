# Compat-Active Candidate Field Liveness

This file defines the compatibility-active form of candidate-field liveness.

Its purpose is:

- make compat-only execution more likely to preserve a small but genuinely alive nearby field
- avoid instant collapse to one route when nearby alternatives are still informative

## Core Claim

Compat-only execution should already allow:

- nearby candidates to remain perceptibly alive
- those candidates to keep weak local pressure on the leading route
- field life to remain real without becoming route sprawl

This is not:

- mandatory multi-route solving

It is:

- local field vitality

## What This Means In Compatibility Form

At the compatibility-active level, candidate-field liveness means:

- a leading route does not immediately erase nearby lawful alternatives
- nearby candidates can survive while they still illuminate live burden
- route quality improves because local competition remains briefly real

## Allowed Compatibility Pressure

The compatibility layer may already preserve:

- `coactivation_density`
- `candidate_field_liveness`
- `cluster_persistence`

These are enough to support:

- weak local field vitality

without:

- visible route display

## Final Rule

Compat-only execution should already let a nearby candidate field stay alive while it is still informative.

It must not expose that field as workflow.
