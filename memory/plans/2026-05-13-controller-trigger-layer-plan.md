**Approach:** Insert a thinner pre-skill trigger layer that scores burden deletion, carrier collapse, boundary pressure, singular crack, symmetry flattening, aggregate control, and representative-value opportunities before ordinary skill competition settles.
**Estimated Total Time:** 70 minutes
